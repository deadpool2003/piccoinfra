Commercial Office

Next-Gen Tech Hub

Workspace for Tomorrow

The Tech Hub project was a comprehensive corporate interior and MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) design-build mission. The goal was to create a vibrant, high-energy environment for a growing technology company that fosters collaboration and innovation.

Our approach integrated ergonomic furniture systems with high-spec MEP infrastructure. We installed advanced HVAC systems for zone-based climate control and high-performance LED lighting that adjusts based on natural light levels throughout the day.

The design includes "Collision Zones"—open lounges and huddle rooms placed strategically between departments—and a massive cafeteria that doubles as an "all-hands" meeting space. Quality materials and precise execution ensure the office is both durable and aesthetically inspiring.

Project Specifications

Category Office Design
