Modern Villa Residency

Luxurious Living Defined

The Modern Villa project is a masterpiece of contemporary architecture and sustainable design. Located in the heart of the city, this private residence was envisioned as a sanctuary that balances bold geometric forms with warm, natural materials.

Our team focused on creating an open-plan layout that maximizes natural light through floor-to-ceiling glass facades. Every corner of the villa has been thoughtfully designed to provide both functionality and a sense of effortless luxury.

The interior palette features premium Italian marble, custom-crafted teak wood elements, and a state-of-the-art smart home integration system that controls everything from lighting to climate with a single touch.

Project Specifications

Category Residential
Location East Coast Road, Chennai
Area 4,500 Sq. Ft.
Duration 14 Months
Completion June 2024
